Every year, 30 chosen bloggers & social media influencers board a luxury bus that takes them on a two-week road trip across God’s Own Country. Organized by the Department of Tourism, Kerala Blog Express provides a handful of international bloggers a unique platform to experience Kerala in full bloom. The itinerary is arduously planned to highlight the myriad experiences and exhaustive range of activities that a small tropical state like Kerala has on offer.

Why Kerala

Kerala, nestled in southern India, captivates travelers as a captivating holiday destination for numerous reasons. Its breathtaking natural beauty, from the serene backwaters to the picturesque beaches and verdant hill stations like Munnar, creates an enchanting backdrop. The rich cultural heritage, vibrant art forms, and diverse traditions add a tapestry of experiences, complemented by the allure of Ayurvedic wellness retreats. The delectable cuisine, featuring flavorful dishes like appam and fish curry, tantalizes the taste buds. Moreover, wildlife sanctuaries and national parks provide glimpses of exotic flora and fauna, while adventure enthusiasts revel in trekking, rafting, and wildlife safaris. Kerala's warm hospitality and diverse accommodation options, from luxurious resorts to eco-friendly homestays, ensure a memorable and comfortable stay. Overall, Kerala beckons visitors with its blend of natural wonders, cultural richness, and immersive experiences, making it an enticing choice for a holiday getaway.Certainly! Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," stands out as a prime holiday destination due to its multifaceted allure. The ethereal beauty of the backwaters, an intricate network of lagoons, canals, and lakes fringed by palm trees, invites travelers to indulge in serene houseboat cruises.
